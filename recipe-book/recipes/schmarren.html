Schmarren

Description

Pronounced [sha-more-ren], this traditional Slovenian dish is similar to pancakes, and always eaten for breakfast when I visit my grandparents! The batter creates light and fluffy dough that can be dusted with powdered sugar to enjoy.

This is a photo of the recipe my great grandmother used in a Slovenian cooking class in 1978, with some of her handwritten notes!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 7/8 cups milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 4 egg whites
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Beat egg yolks and sugar. Add milk, flour, and salt until a smooth paste. Fold in beaten egg-whites into first mixture.
  2. In a large skillet or fry-pan, melt 1 tablespoon butter, and spoon 1/4 of the mixture into the frying pan. (It should not be thinner thann 1/4 inch)
  3. Fry slowly on medium heat and allow to puff. Turn and brown on other side. Do not cook too long as it tends to dry.
  4. Put on platter, then with two forks tear bits of Schmarren into bite-size pieces. Repeat 3 more times for a full batch.
  5. After tearing each batch, be sure to sprinkle with powdered sugar.
