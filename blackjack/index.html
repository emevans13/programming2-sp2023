    The goal is to draw cards that total 21, or come closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. (all face cards are 10)

  1. Once once you hit start, you (the user) and the dealer (the computer) are dealt 2 cards.
  2. You may then decide if you want to "hit." If so, you're dealt another card.
  3. If you do not wish for another card, you can select "stay".
  4. Once you're finished drawing cards, the dealer will either draw cards or stay.
